# Business Problem

Credit card portfolios expose financial institutions to default risk due to customer delinquency, high utilization, and deteriorating credit profiles.

Traditional monitoring processes often lack real-time insights into:
- Which accounts are most at risk
- How much financial exposure is tied to high-risk customers
- Where expected losses are concentrated

This creates delays in:
- Risk mitigation actions
- Credit limit adjustments
- Collections strategy execution

The objective of this project is to build a data-driven credit risk analytics framework that:
- Identifies high-risk accounts
- Quantifies expected loss
- Enables proactive decision-making
