# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

## 1. Strengthen Collections Strategy
- Focus on Default segment accounts (90+ DPD)
- Prioritize high expected loss accounts for recovery

## 2. Implement Early Warning System
- Monitor Watchlist accounts (utilization ≥ 80%)
- Trigger alerts for rapid balance growth

## 3. Adjust Credit Limits
- Reduce exposure for Medium Risk accounts
- Reassess credit policies for high utilization customers

## 4. Risk-Based Monitoring
- Increase review frequency for Medium Risk segment
- Track movement across risk categories

## 5. Portfolio Optimization
- Identify concentration risk in high-loss segments
- Rebalance portfolio exposure where necessary

---

## Strategic Benefit

These actions help:
- Reduce expected losses
- Improve portfolio stability
- Enable proactive risk management
