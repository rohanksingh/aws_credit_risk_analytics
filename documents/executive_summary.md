# Executive Summary

An end-to-end credit risk analytics solution was developed using AWS (S3, Glue, Athena) and Power BI to assess portfolio risk and quantify expected loss.

The analysis segmented accounts based on delinquency and utilization metrics and applied expected loss modeling using PD, EAD, and LGD.

## Key Findings

- High-risk segments (Default and Medium Risk) contribute disproportionately to total expected loss.
- A small number of accounts represent a large portion of portfolio risk.
- Watchlist accounts (high utilization) indicate early-stage risk signals.

## Impact

This solution enables:
- Faster identification of high-risk accounts
- Quantification of financial exposure
- Data-driven credit and collections strategies

The dashboard provides an executive-level view of:
- Total exposure
- Total expected loss
- Risk concentration across segments
