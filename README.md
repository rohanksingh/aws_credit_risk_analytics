
# aws_credit_risk_analytics



The credit card portfolio has rising delinquency risk due to delayed identification of high-risk customers, manual review processes, and limited segmentation based on payment behavior, utilization, and delinquency history.

The goal is to analyze portfolio risk, segment customers, identify process improvement opportunities, and recommend data-driven credit risk actions with measurable financial impact.
